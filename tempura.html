Tempura

Tempura is from Japan. Long ago, Portuguese people taught Japanese to fry food. Today, tempura is a popular dish in Japan and other countries.

    Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Seafood
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Oil

    Cook

  • Firstly: Mix flour, egg, and water to make batter.
  • Secondly: Cut vegetables and seafood.
  • Then: Dip vegetables and seafood into the batter.
  • Next: Fry them in hot oil.
  • Finally: Take out and eat.
