Sushi

Sushi is from Japan. Long ago, people put fish in rice to save it. Later, they started eating the rice with fish. Today, sushi is very popular all over the world

    Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Seaweed
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Vinegar

    Cook

  • Firstly: Cook the rice
  • Secondly: Add vinegar to the rice
  • Then: Put rice on seaweed
  • Next: Put fish on the rice
  • Then: Roll the seaweed with rice and fish
  • Finally: Cut the roll into small pieces and eat with soy sauce and wasabi
