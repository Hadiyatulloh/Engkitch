Ramen

Ramen is from China but very popular in Japan. People in Japan made many types of ramen. Today, ramen is eaten all over the world.

    Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Egg
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Broth

    Cook

  • Firstly: Cook the noodles.
  • Secondly: Prepare the broth with meat and vegetables.
  • Then: Put noodles in a bowl.
  • Next: Pour the broth with meat and vegetables over noodles.
  • Finally: Add boiled egg and eat.
